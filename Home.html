Agency Background

The Agency that proivided this data set is the Department of Health and Mental Hygiene(DOHMH).
The DOHMH are one of the world's largest public health agencies.

DOHMH are respnsible for things such as: - Birth Certificates
- Dog Licenses
- Restaurant Inspection & Enforcement
- Public Health Research
- Disease Prevention
- Health Education
