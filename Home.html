Agency Background

The Agency that proivided this data set is the Department of Health and Mental Hygiene(DOHMH).
The DOHMH are one of the world's largest public health agencies.

DOHMH are respnsible for things such as:
- Birth Certificates
- Dog Licenses
- Restaurant Inspection & Enforcement
- Public Health Research
- Disease Prevention
- Health Education

Importance & Relevance

The importance of this dataset is to show the data on dog bites.
This dataset will also show the dog breed, gender, age, and date that the incident has occureed
