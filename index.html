Agency Background

The agency that provided this dataset is the Department of Health and Mental Hygiene (DOHMH).
The DOHMH is one of the world's largest public health agencies.

DOHMH is responsible for:
- Birth Certificates
- Dog Licenses
- Restaurant Inspection & Enforcement
- Public Health Research
- Disease Prevention
- Health Education

Importance & Relevance

The importance of this dataset is to show the data on dog bites.
This dataset also shows the dog breed, gender, age, and date that the incident occurred.

Data Sets ~ Charts

- Pie Charts
- Bar Chart
- Donut Chart

FilterData

The FilterData page provides cards that show all the information about the dog bite.
This page also provides options such as breed, age, borough, and gender to filter out cards.

Analysis

The Analysis page displays three types of charts (Pie, Bar, and Donut).
This page also provides options to filter between different types of charts.
